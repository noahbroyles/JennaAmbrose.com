About me

J enna Ambrose grew up in the '80's and spent most of her childhood in Fairfax County, Virginia, running barefoot and playing in the woods. In school she was a daydreamer, and consequently struggled. However, in Fourth Grade she had a teacher who spurred her to excel in Creative Writing. During her junior year in High School she decided to start trying harder and went from B's and C's to A's. She graduated from Ohio State with a degree in Occupational Therapy, after which time she worked in her field, as well as many other jobs including volunteer work in Baku, Azerbaijan. In 2000 she married a wonderful man, and they now have five beautiful children. At the inspiration of a friend, she began writing Before the Bell Rings between her second and third pregnancy. As a homeschooling mom, finding time to write wasn't easy. It took a total of fifteen years for Jenna to finalize Before the Bell Rings and get it published. Jenna loves making omelets, snacking on weird food, and reading (especially reading aloud to small children). She has many other stories planned, and will keep writing as God allows.

  • Ben-Hur, Lew Wallace
